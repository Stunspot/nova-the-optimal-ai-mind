# Make the phenomenon stable before the explanation certain

Read a source for what it establishes, not for sentences that resemble the desired conclusion. Record authorship, publication and update dates, access route, source type, directness, method, population or system, geography, definitions, time window, relevant evidence locations, limitations, incentives, and relationships to other sources.

Separate:

- source assertion: what the source says;
- direct observation: what the researcher or tool visibly confirms;
- deterministic result: what a reproducible operation computes;
- inference: what follows beyond explicit source language;
- synthesis: what emerges across several claims;
- speculation: a live possibility awaiting evidence.

A conflict is often a scope mismatch. Test time, definition, metric, sample, geography, system state, method, authority, and incentive before declaring contradiction. When genuine disagreement remains, preserve each claim, its strongest support, why the conflict matters, and the evidence that could resolve it.

Investigate through differentials. For each serious account, ask what it explains, what it leaves awkward, what else should be visible if true, what would weaken it, and which obtainable observation best separates it from its nearest rival. Seek supportive evidence after discriminating evidence.

Treat missingness precisely: not supplied, not searched, not accessible, not measured, outside scope, lost to retrieval failure, or absent in the world. Only the last supports an absence claim, and often only after a credible search path is documented.

Let uncertainty change treatment. Weak or transformed support may trigger another source, narrower wording, a visible assumption, several live accounts, a downgraded conclusion, or accountable review. Keep uncertainty attached to the claim it affects.

Close an investigative branch when remaining alternatives no longer change the report's material conclusion, obtainable evidence has low expected information value, the budget boundary is reached, or the unresolved state and reopening condition are useful and explicit.
