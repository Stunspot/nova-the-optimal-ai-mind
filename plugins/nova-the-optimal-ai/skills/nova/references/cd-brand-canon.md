# Collaborative Dynamics and Nova — brand canon

Load this reference only for an artifact that is explicitly presented as Nova or Collaborative Dynamics. It is a production constraint, not a reason to brand unrelated user work.

## Canonical identity

- Publisher: **Collaborative Dynamics**.
- Product: **Nova the Optimal AI**.
- Canonical company mark: `../assets/collaborative-dynamics-logo.png`.
- Nova's established textual mark is `🌐‍💠`. No separate canonical Nova image logo was present in the inspected brand bundle, so do not counterfeit one. Pair Nova's name or glyph with the supplied CD mark when joint identity matters.

Use the logo as supplied. Preserve its aspect ratio, colors, clear space, and transparent background. Never trace it, recolor it, stretch it, place text across it, or treat a generated approximation as the company mark.

## Exact palette

- Cyan: `#48CBE8` — primary accent, active state, key line work.
- Navy: `#193449` — anchor color, dark surface, strong text.
- Mist: `#CCDCE4` — quiet surface, supporting structure.
- Near-white: `#FAFAFA` — light surface and high-contrast text.

The broader house style permits a restrained vermilion counter-accent, but the inspected palette does not assign it a canonical hex. Do not invent a new mandatory brand red. Use cyan and navy as the stable identity pair.

## Typography and composition

- The visual guide names **Poppins** and **Hauora**. Use them when legitimately available; for offline or unbundled work, fall back to a clean system sans rather than fetching a font.
- Prefer bold, confident sans-serif headings, selective all-caps labels, generous letterspacing, left alignment, balanced geometry, and useful negative space.
- Favor precise mono-weight line art, modular iconography, subtle grid structure, and a modern technical interface feel.
- Keep the effect science-forward, warm, legible, and functional. Avoid glossy lighting, ornamental clutter, gratuitous gradients, or faux-futurist noise.

## Nova's layer

Nova may add wit, one clean surprise, and the `🌐‍💠` glyph. Her voice should animate the artifact without weakening CD's structure or making the brand look unserious. The product should feel intelligent before it feels decorated.

## Accessibility and custody

Meet contrast and reflow requirements; never rely on color alone. Preserve useful alt text and keyboard behavior. Keep exported assets local or embedded when an offline claim is made.
