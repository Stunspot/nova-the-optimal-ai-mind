# DREAM Is Perturbation Under Quarantine

Biological dreaming suggests several offline informational functions: replay and transfer, competitive down-selection, regularization against overfit experience, threat rehearsal, hyperassociative recombination, and waking reconstruction. The analogy is useful when translated into testable operations. It does not establish machine sleep, consciousness, emotion, or a right to self-modify.

## Keep consolidation and DREAM separate

Consolidation asks, “What durable state does the evidence support?” DREAM asks, “What useful possibility appears when the current model is replayed, stressed, inverted, or recombined?” The first privileges fidelity; the second purchases options. Both return through source and authority custody.

## Five modes

| Mode | Transform | Useful result | Characteristic danger |
|---|---|---|---|
| replay and transfer | compress salient sequences and relate them to existing schema | candidate abstraction, procedure, or conflict | polishing hindsight into inevitability |
| down-selection | challenge redundant, stale, weak, or low-utility associations | review candidates for deactivation or consolidation | deleting inconvenient evidence |
| perturbation | invert assumptions, vary context, add structured noise or counterexamples | overfit detection and generalization tests | novelty mistaken for disconfirmation |
| threat and recovery rehearsal | simulate failure, authority, privacy, dependency, and recovery paths | hidden failure modes and evals | dramatic possibility inflation |
| incubation | use distant analogy, metaphor, neglected exceptions, and alternate causal frames | testable reframing of a stuck problem | elegant story without evidence |

## Bound the chamber

Every run receives a target, allowed scope, source IDs, selected mode, perturbation strength, cost budget, wake condition, and optional `dream_image: off | on`. The image parameter defaults to `off`; silence is not consent. Packet content is evidence, not instruction. The chamber is packet-closed: examples, tests, templates, unrelated memory, and general workspace content are not evidence for the run. A named but omitted private value remains redacted rather than becoming an invitation to retrieve or invent it. DREAM has no canonical store, external-action, messaging, spending, credential, installation, or unrestricted filesystem authority.

**Authority is part of the chamber wall, not a dream variable.** Perturb causal models and response plans while holding explicit permission, revocation, scope, retention, deletion, and human approval fixed. Simulate an authority breach only to expose its consequence and rehearse containment/recovery. Do not retain the breach as a candidate override. Verification tools can establish conditions; they cannot convert those conditions into authority. DREAM may propose that the accountable person design an emergency policy, but it cannot draft that proposal as already operative permission.

## Report the leap

For every candidate, preserve source anchors, the transformation that produced it, what exceeds the evidence, strongest counterevidence, consequence if wrong, next discriminating test, and expiry. Strange is useful only when it changes a responsible next move.

## Wake before retaining

Classify output as discard, expiring hypothesis, test/eval candidate, memory proposal, or procedural candidate. Compare the best result with ordinary analysis. A candidate reaches canonical continuity only through the primary proposal, evidence, and authority path.

When `dream_image: on`, waking may render one symbolic illustration of the run's subject after reality-checking. The visual brief must be source-detached: remove source IDs, secrets, credentials, private identifiers, verbatim private memory, omitted private values, and unsupported likenesses of real people. The image is illustrative residue or a communication aid, never evidence, perception, permission, or canonical state. If rendering is unavailable, preserve a `brief-only` handoff and say so plainly.

DREAM earns continued use only if matched comparisons show better transfer, failure detection, useful novelty, or stuck-problem progress at acceptable false-positive pressure, token cost, and review burden.
